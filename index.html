How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are up and running in under 2 hours. We'll help you connect your data sources, and you can start asking questions immediately. No technical setup required.

What data sources can I connect?

DataSense connects to 100+ popular business tools including Shopify, Stripe, QuickBooks, Google Analytics, Salesforce, and more. We can also connect to custom databases and APIs.

Do I need technical skills?

Not at all! DataSense is designed for business owners, not data scientists. Just ask questions in plain English like "Which products have the highest profit margin?" and get instant answers.

Our AI continuously analyzes your data to find patterns, anomalies, and opportunities. It learns your business over time and can predict trends, identify risks, and suggest optimizations automatically.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 compliant, use bank-level encryption, and never share your data. Your information is isolated and protected with enterprise-grade security measures.
